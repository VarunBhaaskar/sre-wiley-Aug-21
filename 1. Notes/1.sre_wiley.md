### CIDR Notations

10.0.0.0/8 ---> 10.{0-255}.{0-255}.{0-255} ---> 256x256x256 --> 16,777,216
172.16.0.0/16 --> 172.16.{0-255}.{0-255} ---> 256x256 ---> 65,536
192.168.0.0/24 --> 192.168.0.{0-255} ---> 256
172.16.5.6/32  --> 172.16.5.6
0.0.0.0/0 --> All the IPs the world (IPv4)
::0 --> All the IPs the world (IPv6)
